FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about OHIP billing and IntelAGENT

What is the best OHIP billing software for Ontario physicians?

IntelAGENT is a leading OHIP billing software used by over 1,500 Ontario physicians. It allows you to submit claims in as little as 15 seconds via mobile app or web portal, with features like health card scanning, offline mode, and real-time revenue tracking. Pricing starts at 0.25% of paid claims with a $39.99/month maximum.

How much does OHIP billing software cost?

IntelAGENT offers two pricing tiers: Personal plan at 0.25% of paid claims (maximum $39.99/month), and Premium plan at 2% of paid claims which includes a dedicated billing agent.

Can I submit OHIP claims from my phone?

Yes, IntelAGENT offers iOS and Android mobile apps that allow you to submit OHIP claims from anywhere. The app includes health card scanning with your camera, offline mode for areas without internet, and syncs automatically when connected.

How long does it take to submit an OHIP claim with IntelAGENT?

With IntelAGENT, physicians can submit OHIP claims in as little as 15 seconds on average. Smart templates remember your common procedures, and health card scanning eliminates manual data entry.

What specialties does IntelAGENT support?

IntelAGENT supports all medical specialties that bill OHIP, including family medicine, internal medicine, psychiatry, surgery, pediatrics, obstetrics, anesthesiology, radiology, and more. The software includes specialty-specific billing code suggestions and templates.

Can IntelAGENT integrate with my EMR?

Yes, IntelAGENT offers EMR integration for clinics and hospital departments. Patient data can flow directly from your EMR to IntelAGENT for streamlined billing. Contact us to discuss integration with your specific EMR system.

How do I get started with IntelAGENT?

Getting started is simple: sign up for a free account at app.intelagent.ca, enter your OHIP billing number, and start submitting claims. Your first 3 months are free with no credit card required. Download our mobile app from the App Store or Google Play to bill on-the-go.
