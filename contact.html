FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long is the free trial?
You get 60 days free to try IntelAGENT. The trial gives you full access to all features so you can see exactly how it works before committing.
Do I need to install software?
No installation needed for the web portal—just log in from any browser. The mobile app is available on the App Store and Google Play.
How do I migrate from another billing service?
We support bulk import via Excel/CSV. If you have existing claim data, you can upload it directly. Our team can also help with migration—just reach out.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We follow healthcare industry best practices for data security and privacy.
