FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the 3-month free trial work?
Sign up and use IntelAGENT completely free for 3 months. At the end of your trial, you can choose to continue with a paid plan or cancel with no obligation.
How is the 0.25% fee calculated?
The fee is calculated based on your total paid claims for the billing cycle. For example, if you receive $10,000 in paid claims, your fee would be $25. The maximum monthly fee is capped at $39.99, regardless of your billing volume.
Can I switch between plans?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. If you upgrade mid-cycle, you'll have immediate access to Premium features.
What's included with the dedicated billing agent?
Your dedicated billing agent reviews all your claims before submission, optimizes billing codes to maximize revenue, handles rejections and appeals, and provides monthly consultations to discuss your billing performance.
Is there a contract or commitment?
No contracts or long-term commitments. Both plans are billed monthly and you can cancel anytime. Your data remains accessible for export even after cancellation.
